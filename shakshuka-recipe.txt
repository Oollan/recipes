Ingredients:
 - 2 tomatoes
 - 1 tomato sauce can
 - 1 onion
 - 2 cloves of garlic
 - 4 eggs
 - salt and pepper

Directions:

 1. Oil a frying pan and heat him up.
 2. Cut the onion into cubes and fry them until they get a gold color.
 3. Peel the tomatoes and cut them into cubes.
 4. Mash the cloves of garlic.
 5. Add the tomatoes, the tomato sauce and the cloves of garlic into the frying golden onions.
 6. Wait 5 minutes.
 7. Add the eggs and cover the frying pan.
 8. Wait another 5 minutes.
 9. Add salt and pepper for your own flavor.
 10. Enjoy the meal!
